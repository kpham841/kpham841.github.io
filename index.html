About

     I'm a fourth-year Cognitive Science w/ Specialization in Design and Interaction major with a Computer Science and Design minor at the University of California, San Diego. I'm interested in web and software development, as well as UI/UX.


     Most of the projects I've done have been web applications. This exposed me to a plethora of languages and technologies, such as ReactJS, MongoDB, Firebase, JavaScript, and HTML/CSS.


      My projects mostly revolve around social good. It brings me great fulfillment to create something that is able to help many people. In the future, I hope to work on projects that will work towards solving the world's biggest problems.


     When I'm not working on side projects, I love learning new skills and technologies (and hopefully incorporating them into future projects). I also dabble a bit in computer and keyboard customization.
